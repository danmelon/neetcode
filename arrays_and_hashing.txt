Arrays & Hashing:

What is it?
    > Arrays sotre elements in contiguous memory, allowing O(1) index access.
    > Hashing maps keys to indicies via a hash function, enabling near-instant lookups.
    > In Python, lists are dynamic arrays and dict/sets are hash tables.

Core idea:
    > When a brute-force solution involves nested loops O((n)^2), ask:
        > 'Can I store something in a hash map to avoid the inner loop?
